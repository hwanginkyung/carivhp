Thank you for visiting the Korea Used Car Export Association.

Greeting

Hello.
I am Park Younghwa, the 8th president of the Korea Used Car Export Association.
We are a member-centered incorporated association and currently export over 300,000 used cars annually to about 160 countries worldwide.
Based on localization strategies and global export competitiveness tailored to each country, we continue to strengthen domestic export infrastructure as well as human, material, and eco-friendly competitiveness.
We also extend our sincere gratitude to all members who work on the front lines amid rapid market changes.

Respecting the will of our members, we are moving forward together with goals such as developing an integrated logistics complex, introducing a one-stop system, and building an eco-friendly logistics framework.
Through this, we will lay the foundation for a more advanced export environment, maximize used-car exports, and raise pride among members and industry workers, contributing to national export growth.

We always welcome visits from members and related industry participants with an open mind and a low threshold, and we promise to listen to your advice and opinions.

With active participation and encouragement from members, we will show a continually growing and advancing association.
During my term, we will pursue eco-friendly logistics complexes, high value-added systems, financial independence, and new overseas market development to ease your burdens and build an association that grows together in a better environment.

Thank you.
