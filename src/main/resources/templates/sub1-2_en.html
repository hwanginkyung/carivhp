Setting a new standard for used-car exports through global trust and innovation.

Purpose of Establishment

The Korea Used Car Export Association was established to resolve structural inefficiencies and information asymmetry in Korea’s used-car export industry and to transform an export structure once reliant on individual businesses into a standardized, systemized, and eco-friendly industry ecosystem.

Joint build-out of export infrastructure such as logistics, customs, and shipping

Establish transaction and operating standards and improve transparency

Develop joint strategies to respond to global market changes

Beyond individual competitiveness, we aim to raise industry-wide trust and sustainability.
We also seek to build institutional and structural foundations so the used-car export industry becomes a strategic sector that supports national export competitiveness amid rapid changes in international trade, environmental regulations, and digital transformation.

Vision

Complete used-car exports as an industry

We aim to establish used-car exports as a global industry built on trust, standards, and sustainability—not just volume.

Connect, standardize, and grow

We build integrated export platforms and infrastructure that connect domestic and global markets, and foster long-term, stable growth through eco-friendly logistics and high value-added systems.

Become the standard beyond price

We aim to be a central organization that helps the Korean used-car export industry become a global benchmark recognized for quality, transparency, and operational excellence—providing members with a sustainable business foundation and the nation with a trusted export future.
