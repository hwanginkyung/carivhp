Sponsorship

The association runs public-interest projects to promote the healthy development of the used-car export industry and a fair market environment.
Donations are used for public-purpose programs and initiatives that strengthen the industry foundation.

How donations are used

  • Policy research and regulatory improvements for the used-car export industry
  • Support for export infrastructure and joint systems
  • Eco-friendly and ESG-based export environment initiatives
  • Industry information sharing and education/research
  • Operation of public-purpose programs
