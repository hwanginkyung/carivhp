Partners

The association collaborates with specialized institutions and companies across export, customs, logistics, finance, and IT to build a stable and efficient export environment. Beyond simple partnerships, we operate a strategic collaboration network that strengthens trust and sustainability across the industry.

Logistics, transport, and shipping partners

Logistics & Shipping Partners

We support stable operations across inland transport, export yard storage, port delivery and loading, and international ocean shipping.

Export, customs, and administrative partners

Export & Regulatory Partners

We support administrative tasks such as export declarations, vehicle deregistration, and customs procedures, minimizing legal and regulatory risks for smooth exports.

Vehicle evaluation, inspection, and maintenance partners

Inspection & Vehicle Services Partners

We ensure export vehicle quality through performance evaluation, quality inspection, and maintenance, building trust with overseas buyers.

Finance, payment, and guarantee partners

Finance & Transaction Partners

We provide overseas payments, FX, trade finance, insurance, and guarantees to support safe and stable transactions.

IT, platform, and system partners

Technology & Platform Partners

We support digitalization and automation of export processes through logistics management systems, document automation, and data management.

Overseas network partners

Overseas Network Partners

We leverage overseas buyers and local partner networks to secure stable sales channels and expand globally.
